Privacy Policy


At Staja Health LLC., your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you visit our website Stajahealth.com and when you opt in to receive SMS messages from us.

1. Website Use

When you use our website or sign up to receive SMS messages, we do not collect any personal information. We do not store or save any information besides your phone number and we don’t share this information with any third party or any other outside partner or entity not part of our organization.

2. How We Use Your Information or Phone Number

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To send you SMS messages you have opted in to receive.
  • To respond to inquiries or requests.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. SMS Messaging and Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages from us. These may include service updates, alerts, promotions, or other relevant information. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying “STOP” to any message you receive from us. You may also contact us directly to be removed from our SMS list.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

SMS opt-in and phone numbers collected for SMS communication purposes will not be shared with any third party and affiliates for marketing purposes.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers or partners who help us operate our website and messaging systems.
  • Law enforcement or other authorities if required by law.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies before providing any information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for SMS communications.

To exercise your rights, contact us at referrals@stajahealth.com

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on the type of communication. For example, you may receive up to 5 SMS messages per week related to your meal deliveries.
